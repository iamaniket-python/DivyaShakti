आरंभ से आज तक

Ek Diye Se Shuru Hua Safar

Divya Shakti Darbar ki sthaapna ek chhote se mandir-kaksh se hui thi, jahan mutthi bhar shraddhalu nitya aarti ke liye ikattha hote the. Samay ke saath, vahi diya ek poore darbar mein badal gaya — jahan aaj vedic acharya, shuddh samagri aur shastra-sammat vidhi ke saath, desh-videsh ke shraddhaluon tak puja pahunchti hai.

Hamara vishwas hai ki puja sirf ek riwaz nahi, ek sankalp hai — aur har sankalp ko poori nishtha, shuddhata aur samay ki pabandi ke saath nibhaya jaana chahiye.
