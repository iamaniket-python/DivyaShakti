दरबार परिचय

Ek Darbar, Jahan Aastha Ko Milta Hai Vidhi

Divya Shakti Darbar ek aisa manch hai jahan pramanik vedic acharya, shuddh samagri aur shastra-sammat vidhi ke saath aapke ghar tak puja pahunchti hai — chahe aap kahin bhi ho.

पूजा सेवाएँ

Apni Puja Chunein

Har seva anubhavi purohit dwara, shuddh samagri ke saath sampann karayi jaati hai.

🕉️

Rudrabhishek

Bhagwan Shiv ko jal, dudh aur bilvapatra se abhishek — shanti aur samriddhi ke liye.

Samay: 1.5 ghante
🔥

Navgrah Shanti Havan

Nau grahon ki asaman peeda ko shant karne wali vidhi, jeevan mein santulan ke liye.

Samay: 2 ghante
🪔

Griha Pravesh Puja

Naye ghar mein prem, samridhi aur suraksha ke sankalp ke saath pravesh vidhi.

Samay: 2.5 ghante
📿

Durga Saptashati Path

Maa Durga ki stuti mein saat sau shlokon ka anushthan, shakti-jagran ke liye.

Samay: 3 ghante
🐍

Kaal Sarp Dosh Nivaran

Kundli mein kaal sarp yog ke prabhav ko kam karne ki vishesh puja.

Samay: 2 ghante
🌺

Maa Lakshmi Aarti

Dhan, samriddhi aur sthirta ke liye Diwali evam nitya aarti anushthan.

Samay: 45 minute
Sabhi Sevayein Dekhein Seva Book Karein

भक्तों के अनुभव

Shraddhaluon Ke Vachan

“Rudrabhishek itni shraddha aur shuddhata se hua, laga jaise sakshaat mandir mein baithe hon.”

— Meenal Sharma, Lucknow

“Griha Pravesh ki vidhi bahut vistaar se aur samay par sampann hui. Sukoon mila.”

— Rakesh Verma, Bhopal

“Navgrah Havan ke baad jeevan mein sach mein shanti mehsoos hui. Dhanyavaad Darbar.”

— Sunita Pandey, Patna

“Purohit ji ka gyan aur vidhi dono hi bahut praman-sammat the.”

— Arvind Tiwari, Varanasi

Apni Puja Ka Sankalp Aaj Hi Lein

Shubh muhurat, shuddh vidhi aur anubhavi acharya — sab kuch ek jagah.
